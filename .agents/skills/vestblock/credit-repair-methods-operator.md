# Credit Repair Methods Operator

Use this when expanding VestBlock credit repair education, dispute guidance, letter types, or customer-facing method explanations.

## Safe method priority

Prefer methods tied to:

- incomplete or inaccurate reporting
- direct bureau disputes
- direct furnisher disputes
- debt validation
- method of verification follow-up
- identity theft blocks and fraud alerts
- personal info and mixed-file correction
- outdated reporting review
- utilization and profile cleanup

## Avoid overclaiming

Do not present any method as:

- guaranteed deletion
- guaranteed score increase
- a legal loophole
- a way to remove accurate information just because it is negative

## Good product framing

- “Use this when the reporting looks inaccurate, incomplete, or unverifiable.”
- “Best after a verified dispute result that still appears wrong.”
- “Useful when identity theft is involved and the account is not yours.”
- “This is an educational and workflow support tool, not legal advice.”

## Methods many consumers do not know well

1. Direct dispute with the furnisher
2. Statement of dispute after an unresolved dispute
3. Reinsertion notice / reappearance review
4. Identity theft block rights
5. Mixed-file and personal-information correction

## Good source types

- CFPB
- FTC
- FCRA / U.S. Code
- eCFR

Keep copy grounded in actual consumer rights and actual VestBlock product behavior.
