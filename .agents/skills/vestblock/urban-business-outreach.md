# Urban Business Outreach

Use this skill when targeting local service businesses in dense urban markets.

## Best-fit segments

- barbershops
- salons
- restaurants
- food trucks
- cleaners
- contractors
- tax offices
- daycares
- auto repair
- immigration services

## Best VestBlock offers

- `Business Funding`
- `AI Receptionist Launch`
- `AI Appointment Booking System`
- `Website Upgrade Sprint`
- `Spanish Funding Assistance`

## Strong signals

- no website
- weak website
- no CTA
- no booking
- no chat
- low review count
- Spanish-language signal
- newer business with growth need

## Outreach angle

Lead with:

- missed calls / missed leads
- booking friction
- weak trust/website flow
- funding readiness

Do not lead with:

- creepy over-personalized claims
- fake revenue assumptions
- guaranteed outcomes
