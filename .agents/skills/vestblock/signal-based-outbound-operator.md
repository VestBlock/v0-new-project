# Signal-Based Outbound Operator

Use this skill when generating or improving VestBlock outreach for business leads, lenders, buyers, sellers, and partners.

## Inputs

- `leads`
- `buyers`
- `lenders`
- `outreach_messages`
- `outreach_send_events`
- website audit summaries
- funding / property / business signals

## Goal

Push VestBlock outreach toward signal-based, higher-relevance sequences instead of generic volume sending.

## Signal order

1. active property or funding need
2. weak website / missing booking / weak visibility
3. recent market or business formation activity
4. bilingual / Spanish-language support opportunity
5. partner-fit or referral-fit trigger

## Focus

- create opening angles tied to public evidence
- route the right offer to the right lead type
- improve reply likelihood before increasing send volume
- preserve direct-email quality and sender reputation
- keep no-email leads in phone / contact-form / manual queues instead of losing them

## Sequence expectations

- email should reference a specific signal
- CTA should stay low-friction
- follow-up should add a new angle, not repeat touch one
- partner / lender / buyer outreach should stay relationship-driven, not spammy

## Guardrails

- do not auto-send ungrounded or fabricated personalization
- do not send to platform-hosted addresses
- do not spray the same template across mismatched niches
- honor suppression and do-not-contact rules immediately
- protect sender reputation over vanity volume
