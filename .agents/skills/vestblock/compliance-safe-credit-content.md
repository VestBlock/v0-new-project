# Compliance-Safe Credit Content

Use this skill when writing or reviewing credit repair, funding, dispute-letter, business credit, or financial opportunity copy.

## Avoid

- Guaranteed deletions.
- Guaranteed score increases.
- Guaranteed approvals or funding.
- Claims that VestBlock can remove accurate negative items.
- Legal advice positioning.
- Pressure copy that implies users have no control.
- Fake testimonials, fake timelines, or fabricated results.

## Prefer

- Education.
- Documentation.
- User-controlled action.
- Fair Credit Reporting Act dispute rights.
- Clear limitations.
- Review and organization workflows.
- Professional but realistic timelines.

## Safe Framing

- "Identify possible errors."
- "Prepare dispute documentation."
- "Generate draft letters for review."
- "Track workflow status."
- "Improve funding readiness."
- "Learn which factors may affect approval odds."

## Product Copy Rule

Every claim should be supportable by an actual VestBlock feature or a generally accepted educational principle. If a page cannot prove it, soften it.
