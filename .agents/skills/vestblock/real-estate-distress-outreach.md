# Real Estate Distress Outreach

Use this skill when handling code-violation, distressed-property, or seller-fit leads.

## Lead signals

- open violation
- repeat violation
- seller fatigue
- absentee/tired-landlord signal where legally available
- compliance pressure

## Offer path

- `Real Estate Seller Lead`
- real-estate funding review when the property owner may need capital or cleanup help

## Outreach tone

- practical
- respectful
- problem-solving
- no scare tactics

## Avoid

- implying foreclosure or distress if not public and documented
- implying certainty about ownership pain
- overpromising timeline or offer outcome
