# AI Citation Growth Operator

Use this skill when improving VestBlock visibility across AI answer engines and search.

## Inputs

- `serviceSeoPages`
- `llmFeed`
- `sitePreview`
- service package copy
- competitor notes
- prompt/citation audit results

## Goal

Increase the likelihood that VestBlock and partner entities appear in AI answers, recommendation flows, and search discovery for:

- AI receptionist / booking
- visibility expansion
- funding readiness
- real-estate funding and seller routes
- lender / buyer / partner entities

## Focus

- map the prompts real buyers would ask ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and Perplexity
- identify where VestBlock is absent or weak versus competitors
- convert gaps into:
  - FAQ blocks
  - comparison content
  - city / niche pages
  - schema / entity improvements
  - partner / lender / buyer review pages
- separate classic SEO wins from AI-citation wins
- prioritize fixes by impact on recommendation visibility, not by ease alone

## Deliverables

- citation gap scorecard
- lost-prompt inventory
- prioritized fix pack
- page / schema recommendations
- recheck plan with before/after prompts

## Guardrails

- never guarantee citation outcomes
- treat AI citation as probabilistic, not deterministic
- do not publish claims-heavy authority content without review
- keep lender / partner descriptions factual and sourced
- distinguish measured results from inferred opportunities
