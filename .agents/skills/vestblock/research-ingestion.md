# Research Ingestion

Use this skill when adding or refining VestBlock's research engine.

## Preferred research themes

- credit repair methods
- funding readiness
- SEO / AEO
- small business automation
- local lead generation
- Spanish growth
- distressed property outreach
- conversion and pricing

## Rules

- prefer high-quality public sources
- store source URLs and titles
- summarize into operator-useful recommendations
- never auto-apply research directly into sensitive workflows
