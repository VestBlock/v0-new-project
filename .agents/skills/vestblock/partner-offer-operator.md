# Partner Offer Operator

Use this skill when adding or maintaining VestBlock partner offers, affiliate links, business funding referrals, credit builder resources, or side-hustle opportunities.

## Offer Categories

- Business funding.
- Business credit.
- Grants and opportunities.
- Credit builder tools.
- Secured cards.
- Rent reporting.
- Education resources.
- Partner/referral links.

## Data Rules

- Track source page, CTA, lead type, and user email when authenticated.
- Use `leads` for funding and referral interest when available.
- Do not claim guaranteed approvals.
- Disclose affiliate/referral relationships where appropriate.
- Keep partner offers separate from credit analysis conclusions.

## Admin Follow-Up

- New funding lead should appear in admin.
- Admin should see lead status, notes, source, and created date.
- High-intent leads can trigger email alerts.
- Paid users and uploaded-report users should be prioritized for follow-up.

## Content Rules

- Position offers as options, not promises.
- Explain requirements, risks, fees, and suitability.
- Avoid predatory language or urgency that pressures users into debt.
- Link back to user-controlled actions: upload report, analyze credit, prepare funding readiness.

## Future Automation

- Lead scoring.
- Partner payout tracking.
- Referral status updates.
- Follow-up task creation.
- AEO pages tied to specific offer categories.
