# Funding Strategy Improvement

Use this skill when reviewing VestBlock funding recommendations and readiness logic.

## Inputs

- `funding_profiles`
- `funding_recommendations`
- `funding_sequence_items`
- approvals and stall patterns

## Goal

Identify where users stall, where readiness guidance is weak, and where offer sequencing should improve.

## Guardrails

- do not auto-guarantee outcomes
- queue meaningful strategy changes for approval
- keep recommendations framed as estimates and preparation guidance
