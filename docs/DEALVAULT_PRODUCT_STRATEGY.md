# DealVault Product Strategy

## Positioning

DealVault by VestBlock is a blockchain-backed agreement, payout, and milestone tracking layer for real estate operators and adjacent partner workflows.

Primary audiences:

- investors
- wholesalers
- contractors
- creative finance operators
- referral partners

## Core Value

DealVault helps operators:

- prove when agreements were created
- track referral and JV payout splits
- keep a clean audit trail
- attach document proof records to deals
- track milestone progress for rehab or contractor work

## MVP Boundaries

MVP is a ledger and proof layer only.

Not included:

- tokenization
- custody
- escrow
- real fund movement

## Pricing Ideas

- Solo investor: `$97/month`
- Team: `$297/month`
- Business: `$997/month`
- Setup/custom workflow: `$997-$5,000`
- Per proof certificate: `$25`
- Future payout tracking fee: `0.5%-1%`

## Rollout Strategy

1. Keep it behind a feature flag inside VestBlock
2. Validate the customer workflow on testnet and off-chain demo mode
3. Launch as an add-on module once proof and deal flows are stable
