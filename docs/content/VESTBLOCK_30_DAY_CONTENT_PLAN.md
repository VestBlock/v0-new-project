# VestBlock 30-Day Content Plan

Goal: build trust and demand while production hosting is paused or limited. This plan favors content that can be posted manually on Facebook, LinkedIn, short-form video, email, PDFs, and partner outreach.

## Weekly Themes

Week 1:

Make VestBlock easy to understand.

Week 2:

Show the problems: weak visibility, slow response, messy records, funding confusion.

Week 3:

Show the proof: PDFs, demo records, flowcharts, before/after examples, screenshots, short videos.

Week 4:

Ask for action: reviews, demos, partner calls, funding prep, AI setup, website review.

## Weekly Production Rhythm

Monday:

Publish one strong educational post.

Tuesday:

Post one short video or founder-facing explanation.

Wednesday:

Send 25-50 reviewed outreach messages if the queue is clean and compliant.

Thursday:

Publish one proof asset: PDF, screenshot, demo record, checklist, or flowchart.

Friday:

Post one direct offer with a clear CTA.

Weekend:

Founder reflection, behind-the-build update, or simple lesson learned.

## Repurposing Rule

Every strong idea should become:

- One Facebook/LinkedIn post
- One short video
- One email
- One website FAQ answer
- One outreach angle
- One future SEO page section
