# VestBlock Content Command Center

This is the source of truth for premium VestBlock content while production hosting is paused. Use it for website copy, sales PDFs, pitch deck language, social posts, outbound emails, PR pitches, AI-search pages, and founder-led sales conversations.

## The Core Message

VestBlock helps serious small businesses become easier to find, faster to contact, and easier to trust.

That is the simplest buyer-facing explanation. Everything else should support one of those three outcomes:

- Get found: Search Visibility helps buyers discover the business in Google, local search, and AI-style answers.
- Respond faster: AI Receptionist helps the business capture, qualify, and route serious inquiries before they go cold.
- Prove the important stuff: DealVault helps teams organize agreement records, payout visibility, milestone history, and proof certificates.

## The Big Problem

Most businesses do not lose revenue in one obvious place. They lose it through quiet leaks:

- The right buyer cannot find them.
- A good lead waits too long for a response.
- Important agreement details live across texts, PDFs, emails, screenshots, and memory.
- Funding or growth opportunities get delayed because the business is not organized enough to move quickly.

VestBlock sells the fix for those leaks.

## Factory Workflow

Use this workflow when turning one VestBlock idea into multiple sales assets:

1. Idea queue: choose one buyer pain, not a random topic.
2. Brief: define buyer, problem, outcome, proof, CTA, and compliance limits.
3. Draft: create the main asset first, such as a page section, email, PDF, deck slide, or short video script.
4. Compliance review: remove guarantees, fake proof, overclaiming, and confusing technical language.
5. Publish or package: place it on the website, in `public/proof/`, `public/sales/`, or a sales doc.
6. Repurpose: turn the same core idea into a social post, outreach angle, FAQ, and visual caption.
7. Performance note: record whether the asset supports visibility, outreach, conversion, or funding conversations.

## The Content Standard

Every piece of VestBlock content should answer four questions fast:

1. What problem is this solving?
2. Who is it for?
3. What changes after they buy?
4. What should they do next?

If the content does not answer those questions, rewrite it before publishing.
