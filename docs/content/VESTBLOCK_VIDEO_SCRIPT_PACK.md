## Video 2: DealVault Explained Simply

Length:

60 seconds

Hook:

> DealVault is smart contracts without making your customers feel like they joined a crypto project.

Voiceover:

DealVault is for teams that need cleaner records around agreements, referrals, payouts, approvals, and milestones.

Here is the simple version.

You keep the private document private.

DealVault creates a hash, which is like a fingerprint for that exact file.

Then the record can connect that proof to a deal, a payout split, a milestone, or a certificate.

That means the business can show a clearer trail of what happened without publishing the actual contract or private details.

It does not replace attorneys, escrow, title, or written agreements.

It adds a better proof layer for serious business records.
