# VestBlock InVideo AI Prompt Pack

Use these prompts in InVideo AI to create social videos, pitch clips, and short ads. The style is intentionally premium: fintech, AI business systems, smart proof records, no crypto gimmicks, no fake claims.

## Usage Notes

- Use vertical format for Reels, Shorts, TikTok, and Facebook posts.
- Use square or horizontal only for LinkedIn, pitch deck embeds, and website hero clips.
- Keep captions on by default.
- Choose professional corporate, cinematic tech, or calm futuristic music.
- Avoid fake testimonials, fake customer names, fake stats, and any guarantee language.
- Do not claim guaranteed rankings, sales, funding approvals, legal enforceability, escrow, custody, or automatic payouts.
