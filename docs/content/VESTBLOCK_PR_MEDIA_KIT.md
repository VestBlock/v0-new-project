## Press Release Draft

Headline:

VestBlock Launches Practical Growth And Proof Tools For Small Businesses

Subheadline:

The company combines search visibility, AI receptionist setup, funding preparation, website upgrades, and DealVault proof records to help businesses become easier to find, contact, and trust.

Body:

Brookfield, Wisconsin - VestBlock is building a practical business technology platform for small businesses that need stronger visibility, faster lead response, and cleaner records around important business activity.

The company focuses on four immediate problems: buyers cannot find the business, serious inquiries go unanswered too long, funding preparation is disorganized, and important agreement or milestone details are scattered across emails, PDFs, texts, and screenshots.

VestBlock's current services include Search Visibility, AI Receptionist setup, Website Upgrade Sprints, Business Funding Prep, and DealVault, a proof-record product designed to help teams organize agreement records, referral payout visibility, milestone history, and proof certificates.

"Most small businesses do not need more noise," said Rob Sanders, founder of VestBlock. "They need clearer pages, faster response, and better proof around the work that matters. VestBlock is being built around those practical needs."

DealVault uses blockchain-backed proof concepts carefully. Private documents stay private, and the product is positioned as a record and accountability layer. It is not a replacement for legal counsel, escrow, title services, custody, brokerage compliance, or required written agreements.

VestBlock is currently focused on helping service businesses, real estate teams, contractors, funding-related businesses, agencies, and referral-heavy teams improve visibility, follow-up, and proof-ready records.

Call to action:

Business owners can request a visibility review, AI receptionist setup, funding prep review, or DealVault demo through VestBlock.
