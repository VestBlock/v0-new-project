# VestBlock Premium Content System

Start here when creating public content.

## Files

- `VESTBLOCK_CONTENT_COMMAND_CENTER.md`: core positioning, buyer language, voice, hero copy, and product message house.
- `VESTBLOCK_READY_TO_POST_LIBRARY.md`: ready-to-post Facebook, LinkedIn, email, founder, and outreach copy.
- `VESTBLOCK_SEO_AEO_CONTENT_BRIEFS.md`: page briefs designed for Google and AI-search discoverability once hosting is restored.
- `VESTBLOCK_VIDEO_SCRIPT_PACK.md`: short-form video scripts for services, founder-led selling, and product demos.
- `VESTBLOCK_INVIDEO_AI_PROMPTS.md`: copy-ready InVideo AI prompts for 10 premium VestBlock videos.
- `VESTBLOCK_PR_MEDIA_KIT.md`: boilerplate, press release draft, podcast pitch, partner pitch, and media angles.
- `VESTBLOCK_30_DAY_CONTENT_PLAN.md`: daily posting plan and weekly production rhythm.
- `VESTBLOCK_LANDING_PAGE_COPY_PACK.md`: homepage, services, DealVault, AI Receptionist, Search Visibility, Funding Prep, and social preview copy.

## Current Content Thesis

VestBlock should not sound like a pile of unrelated tools. It should sound like one practical business system:

> Make the business easier to find, easier to contact, and easier to trust.

Everything we publish should connect back to that.
