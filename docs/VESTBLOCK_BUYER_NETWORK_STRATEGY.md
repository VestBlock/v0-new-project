# VestBlock Buyer Network Strategy

VestBlock's buyer network is built as an acquisition graph, not a loose investor list.

## Goals

- Build market-by-market buyer coverage for seller, code-violation, tired-landlord, and investor-ready opportunities.
- Store real buy boxes so seller leads can be routed to the right buyer type faster.
- Track buyer relationships like a lightweight acquisitions CRM inside VestBlock.
- Strengthen real-estate monetization by improving disposition speed and fallback options.

## Core buyer categories

### Residential and local operators

- Local cash buyer
- Landlord buyer
- BRRRR buyer
- Fix-and-flip buyer
- Wholesaler buyer
- Creative-finance buyer

### Institutional and scaled buyers

- Hedge-fund buyer
- SFR aggregator
- Build-to-rent buyer

### Property-specific buyers

- Small multifamily buyer
- Land buyer
- Commercial buyer
- Mobile-home-park buyer
- Self-storage buyer
- Mixed-use buyer
- Note buyer

## Relationship stages

- `discovered`
- `researched`
- `outreach_ready`
- `contacted`
- `followup_due`
- `responded`
- `reviewing`
- `active_buyer`
- `dormant`
- `paused`
- `not_a_fit`

## Initial seeding priority

Start with the buyers that help VestBlock move distressed and seller opportunities quickly:

1. Local cash buyers
2. Fix-and-flip operators
3. BRRRR and landlord buyers
4. Wholesaler-friendly buyers
5. Institutional buyers in stronger metros
6. Bilingual or Spanish-capable acquisition teams

## Coverage model

Each buyer should eventually include:

- markets served
- asset types
- occupancy preferences
- distress and code-violation tolerance
- price and ARV ranges
- closing-speed signals
- proof-of-funds signals
- creative-finance openness
- referral and intake notes

## Compliance stance

- VestBlock does not imply guaranteed offers or guaranteed buyer performance.
- Buyer outreach stays review-first unless explicitly approved for sending.
- Public-source discovery only. No scraping of private dashboards or prohibited sources.
- Referral and assignment language should stay accurate to the relationship and local rules.
