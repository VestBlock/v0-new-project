# VestBlock PR And Partner Pitch Angles

Last updated: 2026-05-18

## What Is Safe To Pitch From The Current Product State

These points are supported by the current repo and public-facing product copy:

- DealVault is positioned as a proof-record and operational accountability layer, not a legal replacement, escrow product, custody product, or tokenized ownership story.
- DealVault has documented live Polygon deployment artifacts and a buyer-facing demo path at `/dealvault/demo` plus a public demo record at `/dealvault/demo-record`.
- DealVault messaging already supports proof records, payout visibility, milestone history, timestamps, and keeping sensitive files off-chain.
- VestBlock has a live business funding eligibility flow and a paid prep path framed as readiness support, not approval guarantees.
- VestBlock has live visibility proof assets and a published visibility case study showing crawlability, route recovery, and off-site submission evidence.
- VestBlock has an AI receptionist demo and service language tied to missed-call recovery, intake, qualification, and handoff support.

## Guardrails

Lead with:

- practical systems for trust, visibility, and follow-up
- proof records, milestone history, payout visibility, and off-chain privacy
- funding preparation before lender outreach
- AI/search visibility as discoverability and clarity work
- AI receptionist as missed-call capture and cleaner intake

Avoid:

- fake customer logos, partner logos, or media logos
- guaranteed funding approvals, rates, or timing promises
- legal enforceability claims
- escrow, title, brokerage, or compliance replacement claims
- crypto hype, tokenization, or investment framing

## Copy-Ready Short Hooks

### Local business media

Subject: Wisconsin founder building practical trust and lead-response systems for small businesses

Hook:
VestBlock is a Brookfield-built company focused on small-business problems that are expensive but unglamorous: weak online visibility, slow follow-up, and scattered proof around important business activity. The story works because the offers are concrete: AI receptionist support, business funding prep, and DealVault proof records.

### Fintech newsletters

Subject: A safer small-business funding angle: prepare before you apply

Hook:
VestBlock’s funding-prep message is simple: many owners do not need another lender list first. They need cleaner documents, better timing, and a realistic readiness review before they start applying.

### Real estate operator groups

Subject: A practical proof-record layer for agreements, milestones, and payout visibility

Hook:
DealVault gives smaller operator teams one clearer trail for what was agreed, what proof was submitted, what changed, and what is ready for payout review without exposing private documents on-chain.

### Contractor and service business groups

Subject: Contractors lose deals in two quiet places: missed calls and messy proof

Hook:
VestBlock has a concrete contractor story because it addresses two operational leaks at once: AI receptionist support helps capture after-hours opportunities, and DealVault helps keep cleaner milestone, approval, and proof history once work is underway.

### Small business podcasts

Subject: Podcast idea: the quiet systems leaks hurting small-business growth

Hook:
The useful conversation is not “AI for small business.” It is how small businesses lose growth when they are hard to find, too slow to respond, or unable to show clean proof when trust matters.

### Funding communities

Subject: Workshop idea: what to fix before you apply for business funding

Hook:
VestBlock can contribute an educational session on documents, timing, readiness, and business setup before owners reach out to lenders or programs.

### Partner newsletters

Subject: A safe member resource on visibility, follow-up, or proof-ready records

Hook:
VestBlock fits partner newsletters best when the ask is simple: share one useful checklist or demo resource around a bottleneck the audience already feels.
