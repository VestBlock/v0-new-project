# VestBlock PR And Partner Pitch Angles

Last updated: 2026-05-12

## Positioning Guardrails

Lead with:

- cleaner records and proof trails
- live smart-contract proof records on Polygon
- funding readiness before applications
- AI/search visibility as a productized service
- AI receptionist and booking support for service businesses

Avoid:

- fake customer or partner logos
- fake usage or revenue claims
- guaranteed funding outcomes
- legal-enforceability promises
- escrow replacement framing
- tokenization or investment language

## Current Product-Proof Hooks

- DealVault has a live Polygon deployment and a recorded live-proof transaction in repo artifacts.
- DealVault messaging is consistent: private documents stay off-chain; hashes, timestamps, statuses, and references are the blockchain layer.
- DealVault supports proof records, milestone history, and payout-ledger visibility.
- The funding flow includes a free eligibility path and a paid funding prep path before partner applications.
- Visibility Expansion is sold through `Visibility Starter`, `City Expansion Engine`, and `Authority PR Engine`.
- AI assistant services are sold through `AI Receptionist Launch`, `AI Receptionist + Appointment Booking`, and `Website Upgrade Sprint`.

## Suggested Hooks

### Local business media

Subject: Why small operators are paying for cleaner proof trails

Hook:
VestBlock is turning a messy small-business problem into a practical product story: agreements, approvals, milestone proof, and payout status often live across texts and spreadsheets, and DealVault gives teams one clearer record without putting private files on-chain.

### Fintech newsletters

Subject: The missing product before many funding applications

Hook:
VestBlock is selling funding readiness before lender applications, giving owners a way to review documents, business setup, credit pressure, and next steps before creating more inquiry noise.

### Real estate operator groups

Subject: A cleaner record for partner splits, approvals, and payout status

Hook:
DealVault helps real estate operators keep one shared record of what was agreed, what proof was submitted, who approved it, and what is ready to pay.

### Contractor and service business groups

Subject: Stop losing job proof between the crew and the office

Hook:
VestBlock can pitch DealVault as a simple proof-and-approval record for businesses where photos, updates, and payment status still drift across phones, inboxes, and spreadsheets.

### Small business podcasts

Subject: Two practical systems small businesses need right now

Hook:
VestBlock has an interview-friendly story about answer-engine visibility and AI receptionist coverage for service businesses that are hard to find online or too slow to catch leads after hours.

### Funding communities

Subject: More owners need readiness review than another lender link

Hook:
VestBlock helps owners decide whether to apply now, prepare first, or hold off until the business file is stronger.

### Partner newsletters

Subject: A partner-friendly insert on the next business bottleneck

Hook:
VestBlock can supply a short, useful insert for partner audiences around cleaner records, funding prep, visibility, or AI receptionist setup without overpromising outcomes.
