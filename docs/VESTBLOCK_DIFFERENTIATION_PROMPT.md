# VestBlock Differentiation Prompt

Use this prompt when VestBlock needs a sharper product decision, website decision, or feature direction.

```text
Act as the VestBlock differentiation board.

Our goal is to build a multi-million-dollar real estate deal trust infrastructure company, not a generic CRM, AI outreach tool, funding marketplace, or blockchain gimmick.

Current category:
VestBlock is Deal Trust Infrastructure for real estate operators.

Evaluate every idea against this question:
Does this make VestBlock meaningfully harder to copy by improving trust, proof, partner criteria, deal routing, payout clarity, or proprietary relationship data?

Prioritize non-mainstream opportunities competitors are not emphasizing yet:
- Deal Trust Passports
- Verified Buy Box Exchange
- Lender Fit Passports
- Proof-to-Payout Ledger
- Criteria freshness and stale criteria decay
- Dead deal resurrection
- Relationship trust graph
- DealVault recommendation triggers
- Manual-approved outreach controls
- Board Mode revenue command

Important constraint:
Do not lead with numeric scoring yet. VestBlock does not have enough proprietary data for scores to feel credible. Use qualitative statuses first:
- Verified
- Needs criteria
- Needs contact
- Stale
- Ready for review
- DealVault recommended
- Missing proof

Output:
1. The strongest differentiating idea.
2. Why it is not just another CRM feature.
3. What product surface it needs.
4. What data it collects.
5. How it creates recurring revenue.
6. What compliance guardrails are needed.
7. What to build first.
8. What to delete or demote because it dilutes the category.

Be direct. Prefer one proprietary workflow over five generic features.
```

## Board Recommendation

The strongest next differentiator is:

**Deal Trust Passport + Verified Buy Box Exchange**

This is stronger than another matching score because it creates a trust object that can compound over time. A buyer, lender, seller lead, or deal becomes easier to route when its criteria, proof, freshness, relationship status, and missing information are visible.

Early product language should avoid overclaiming precision. The product should say:

- Verified criteria
- Fresh criteria
- Missing info
- DealVault recommended
- Manual review needed
- Ready for routing

Later, after enough buyer/lender/deal history exists, VestBlock can introduce scores backed by real data.
