Last updated: 2026-05-13

## Positioning Guardrails

Lead with:

- cleaner records and proof trails
- live smart-contract proof records on Polygon
- funding readiness before applications
- AI/search visibility as a productized service
- AI receptionist and booking support for service businesses

Avoid:

- fake customer, partner, or lender logos
- fake usage, revenue, or media-placement claims
- guaranteed funding outcomes
- legal-enforceability promises
- escrow replacement framing
- tokenization or investment language

## Short Hooks

### Local business media

Subject: Why small businesses are paying for cleaner proof trails and missed-lead coverage

Hook:
VestBlock has a timely local-business story because it is not pitching abstract AI or abstract blockchain. It is packaging two practical fixes: DealVault for cleaner proof and status trails, and AI receptionist support for businesses that still miss leads after hours.

### Fintech newsletters

Subject: The missing product before many small-business funding applications

Hook:
VestBlock is selling funding readiness before lender applications, giving owners a structured way to clean up documents, pressure-test timing, and decide whether they should apply now or prepare first.

### Real estate operator groups

Subject: A cleaner record for deal proof, approvals, and payout visibility

Hook:
DealVault helps real estate operators keep one shared trail of what was agreed, what proof was submitted, who approved it, and what is ready for payout review without exposing private files publicly.

### Contractor and service business groups

Subject: Stop losing job proof and new leads between the crew and the office

Hook:
VestBlock can pitch an operations story contractors understand immediately: one place for proof and milestone records on active work, plus AI receptionist coverage so inbound calls and forms stop going cold.

### Small business podcasts

Subject: Two growth systems local businesses actually need right now

Hook:
VestBlock has a clean founder-operator interview around two questions: how does a business get found better in search and AI answers, and how does it stop missing leads when nobody can answer every inquiry live.

### Funding communities

Subject: More owners need readiness review than another lender list

Hook:
VestBlock helps owners figure out whether to apply now, prepare first, or hold off until the business file is stronger, which is more credible than pushing applications too early.

### Partner newsletters

Subject: A safe partner insert on the next business bottleneck

Hook:
VestBlock can provide a short educational insert for partner audiences around cleaner records, funding prep, visibility, or AI receptionist setup without overpromising outcomes.
