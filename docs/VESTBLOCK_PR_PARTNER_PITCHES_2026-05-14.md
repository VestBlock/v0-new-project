# VestBlock PR And Partner Outreach Pitches

Last updated: 2026-05-14

## Current product-state guardrails

Use these pitches with the current VestBlock product reality:

- DealVault is a proof-record, payout-visibility, and milestone-history layer. It is not tokenization, escrow, custody, title, brokerage compliance, or legal advice.
- Smart contract language should stay narrow: proof records, timestamped history, and audit-friendly certificates.
- Business funding prep is readiness support before applications, not a guarantee of approval, rates, or funding outcomes.
- Search visibility and AI visibility should be framed as clearer pages, stronger proof assets, and better discoverability. Do not promise rankings or AI-answer placement.
- AI receptionist positioning should stay tied to missed-call recovery, intake, routing, and booking support, not autonomous replacement claims.

## Short boilerplate

VestBlock helps small businesses become easier to find, easier to contact, and easier to trust through DealVault proof records, business funding prep, search visibility support, and AI receptionist setup.

## Copy-ready pitch angles

### Local business media

Subject:

Local founder building practical systems for small-business trust and visibility

Pitch:

VestBlock is a Brookfield-built business focused on a practical problem set for small businesses: getting found, responding faster, and keeping cleaner proof around important business activity. The company combines search visibility support, AI receptionist setup, business funding prep, and DealVault proof records without leaning on inflated AI or blockchain claims.

### Fintech newsletters

Subject:

Why small businesses should fix readiness before applying for funding

Pitch:

Most business owners hear about funding options long before they are actually prepared for the application process. VestBlock's angle is simple: help owners clean up readiness, supporting records, and timing before they chase capital. That gives fintech audiences something more useful than another list of lenders.

### Real estate operator groups

Subject:

A practical smart-contract use case for operator records and split visibility

Pitch:

DealVault by VestBlock is built for teams that need cleaner agreement records, payout visibility, milestone history, and proof certificates around deals. The positioning is operational, not speculative: better records and clearer proof without putting private documents or sensitive details on-chain.

### Contractor and service business groups

Subject:

The two systems contractors need now: lead capture and milestone proof

Pitch:

Contractors and field-service teams often lose opportunities after hours and then struggle to keep a clean proof trail once work starts. VestBlock can speak to both problems with AI receptionist setup for intake and DealVault for milestone and payout visibility, giving the audience a concrete operations story instead of generic AI messaging.

### Small business podcasts

Subject:

Podcast guest idea: the quiet revenue leaks small businesses miss

Pitch:

VestBlock's founder can talk about three practical leaks that cost small businesses growth: poor visibility, slow follow-up, and scattered records. That creates a grounded conversation around search visibility, AI receptionist setup, funding prep, and proof systems without turning the episode into a product pitch.

### Funding communities

Subject:

Workshop idea: what to fix before you apply for business funding

Pitch:

VestBlock can contribute a practical workshop or newsletter article on business funding prep: what documents to organize, what timing mistakes to avoid, and how owners can improve application readiness before reaching out to lenders or funding programs. This is a better fit than outcome-driven funding promises.

### Partner newsletters

Subject:

Useful member resource on visibility, response time, and proof-ready records

Pitch:

VestBlock is a good partner-newsletter fit when framed as a practical resource for members. Partners can share a short checklist, a visibility review offer, an AI receptionist explainer, or a DealVault demo link depending on the audience's current pain point.
