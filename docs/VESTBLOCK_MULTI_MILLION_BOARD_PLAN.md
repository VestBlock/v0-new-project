# VestBlock Multi-Million Board Plan

## Board Decision

VestBlock should become a real estate deal operating layer, not a broad menu of small business services.

The multi-million-dollar path is not more random services. It is a focused revenue system:

1. Capture real estate deal flow.
2. Structure buyer and lender criteria.
3. Convert active operators into recurring DealVault customers.
4. Add high-ticket setup and network operations support when teams need implementation help.
5. Keep support services available, but only when they increase deal flow, partner quality, or retention.

## Revenue Architecture

Primary revenue lines:

1. DealVault subscriptions
   - Solo Investor: $97/mo
   - Team: $297/mo
   - Business: $997/mo
   - Board role: recurring proof, payout, and milestone layer.

2. DealFlow Growth System
   - Suggested package: $2,500 setup + $997/mo
   - Includes seller intake path, buyer/lender routing, AI receptionist, booking support, and visibility work tied to real estate demand.
   - Board role: higher-ticket implementation for operators who need the system, not just the software.

3. Buyer/Lender Network Buildout
   - Custom pricing
   - Includes buy-box structure, lender criteria, partner relationship stages, and manual-approved outreach.
   - Board role: turns VestBlock into a proprietary relationship and deal-routing data asset.

4. Paid Real Estate Deal Review
   - Starting point: $300
   - Board role: monetizes serious deal review while qualifying larger DealVault or network implementation opportunities.

5. Support services
   - Search Visibility, AI Receptionist, business funding prep, setup, grants, and credit tools.
   - Board role: add-on revenue only. These should not dilute the front-door positioning.

## Current Implementation Changes

Website changes now implemented:

- Homepage hero now positions VestBlock as a real estate deal operating layer for serious operators.
- Homepage primary CTA now pushes DealVault demo instead of only low-intent property submission.
- Homepage now includes a board-approved growth model section.
- How-it-works now describes the operating workflow: capture opportunity, route relationship, prove deal path.
- Pricing now includes scale plans for recurring and higher-ticket revenue.
- DealFlow Growth System now has a dedicated landing page and appears as a first-class services/pricing path.

## What To Stop

- Stop making automated email outreach the default growth engine until deliverability, opt-out handling, and daily quality review are working.
- Stop leading with credit repair, grants, broad business funding, SEO, or AI receptionist.
- Stop treating every service as equal.
- Stop adding pages unless they support seller intake, buyer/lender routing, DealVault, or real estate deal review.

## 90-Day Board Priorities

### First 30 Days

- Manually approve all buyer/lender outreach.
- Improve partner records: buy box, markets, asset types, funding criteria, proof-of-funds status, contact quality, response history.
- Push every qualified real estate operator toward DealVault demo or paid deal review.
- Use pricing page scale plans in sales conversations.

### Days 31-60

- Build buyer and lender trust passports before introducing numeric scoring.
- Add partner portal improvements for criteria refresh and deal-fit updates.
- Create a weekly board review: leads captured, demos requested, paid reviews sold, DealVault subscriptions started, partner criteria updated.
- Package case studies around seller intake, buyer routing, lender fit, and DealVault proof.

### Days 61-90

- Relaunch controlled outreach with manual approval only.
- Build referral partner campaigns for title, escrow, lenders, wholesalers, agents, and investor groups.
- Productize the DealFlow Growth System with a repeatable onboarding checklist.
- Create monthly retention review for DealVault customers.

## Board Metrics

The board should review these weekly:

- Qualified seller submissions
- Qualified funding or lender-fit submissions
- Buyer/lender criteria records created or updated
- DealVault demo requests
- Paid deal reviews sold
- DealVault subscriptions started
- Outreach replies from buyers, lenders, and partners
- Manual outreach approval rate
- Bad-fit lead rate
- Revenue by product line

## Vertical Wedge

VestBlock should concentrate the first wedge on:

- wholesalers and disposition teams
- private lenders
- cash buyers
- fix-and-flip operators
- DSCR and rental investors
- title, escrow, and referral partners

This wedge has repeat deal flow, buyer criteria, lender fit, referral payouts, proof needs, and milestone risk. It is the cleanest path for DealVault and DealFlow Growth System adoption.

## Completed Product Layer

The product now includes:

- Partner Criteria Engine: organizes buyer and lender criteria completeness behind qualitative passport statuses.
- Manual Outreach Readiness: separates approved/manual-safe outreach from drafts and bad-fit records.
- DealVault Conversion Triggers: flags matches where proof, payout, milestone, or routing risk should lead to a DealVault pitch.
- DealFlow Command: admin control room for partner readiness, match routing, DealVault triggers, and revenue targets.
- Proof Asset Library: public sales assets showing seller intake, buyer criteria, lender fit, routing, DealVault proof, and payout/milestone trails.
- DealFlow Growth System: premium public offer for operators ready for the full system.

## Board Veto Standard

If a proposed feature, page, automation, or campaign does not improve deal flow, partner quality, proof records, recurring revenue, or operator retention, it should be delayed or removed.

## Next Product Build

The highest-value next build is partner criteria capture:

- Buyer portal: buy boxes, markets, close speed, proof-of-funds status, asset appetite, contact quality.
- Lender portal: lending criteria, state coverage, minimum credit profile, DSCR appetite, low-doc support, speed to close.
- Admin readiness: partner quality, relationship stage, outreach status, response history, best-fit notes, and stale criteria flags.
- Deal matching: route seller or funding opportunities to the right buyer/lender profile.

This is the strongest product path because it makes VestBlock more valuable with every partner record and every deal reviewed.

## Deepening Decision

The board's next implemented move is to deepen the premium operating-system offer rather than add more small services.

Reason:
- DealFlow Growth System is the bridge between public lead capture and recurring/high-ticket revenue.
- It packages existing assets into a larger business outcome without making prohibited claims.
- It gives serious operators a reason to buy a system, not just submit a form.
- It supports DealVault subscription growth by making DealVault the proof layer inside a wider deal-flow workflow.

## Differentiation Decision

VestBlock should avoid leading with numeric scores until enough proprietary relationship and deal history exists.

The differentiated product language should be:

- Deal Trust Passport
- Verified Buy Box Exchange
- Lender Fit Passport
- Proof-to-Payout Ledger
- Criteria freshness
- Missing-info checklist
- DealVault recommended

Scores can come later. The early product should feel like a trust and criteria system, not a black-box ranking tool.

## Website Positioning Decision

The board should not let Deal Trust Passports, verified buy boxes, or proof assets take over the whole website.

Homepage priority should be:

- Real estate deal operating layer
- Seller property review
- Buyer and lender routing
- DealVault proof and accountability
- How the workflow operates
- Real estate operator audiences
- Trust/passport layer as a supporting differentiator
- Support services and final conversion

The trust layer belongs on the homepage, but lower on the page. It should help serious operators understand why VestBlock is different without distracting first-time visitors from the core real estate path.
